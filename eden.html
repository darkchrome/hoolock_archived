Eden Housing Scholarship Fundraiser

The Howard T. Collins Memorial Scholarship Program at Eden Housing was named in honor of a former board member, Howard T. Collins, who was known for his passion for justice and tireless work to expand opportunity and access to higher education. Each year, Eden Housing awards scholarships to residents of all ages who are pursuing advanced education opportunities.

This year, we're offering a digital collectible to recognize your contribution. This digital collectible is a non-fungible token (NFT) that you can save as a momento for yourself, send to someone or sell if you'd like! In the future, our goal is to provide holders of this token with special benefits, including meetings with our executive team and opportunities to shape the future of Eden Housing.

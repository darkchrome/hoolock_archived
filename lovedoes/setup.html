How to Set Up Coinbase Wallet

To receive or purchase NFTs, you will need a digital wallet. These steps cover the absolute minimum you need to know to set it up.

If this is your first time setting up a wallet, we recommend using Coinbase Wallet. It is a simple browser plug-in, so it's easy to get started fast. Plus Coinbase has excellent educational resources and support in case you ever need it.

Step 1

Visit this link and follow the instructions for downloading and installing Coinbase Wallet. You will need to use the Chrome browser.

Step 2

Once you have a wallet set up, you will see a screen that looks similar to the one below. Click around to familiarize yourself with it. The 'down arrow' button shows you your wallet address, which you can share with people similar to how you would share your email address. Since this is your first time setting up a wallet, you will see that your balance is $0 and you initially have no NFTs.
